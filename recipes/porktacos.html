Pork Tacos

Description

Pork Tacos are a easy to make, yet delicious dish that will impress anyone you would want to cook for. As opposed to a classic beef taco, the pork taco is typically made with a soft shell, making for less of a mess for the person eating it. They can be made with pulled or ground pork, but for this recipe we will be focusing on ground pork with a topping of pickled onions, kale, and feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 pounds ground pork
  • Tex Mex seasoning
  • 1/8 pound uncooked kale
  • Feta cheese (to taste)
  • 2 red onions, thinly sliced
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Regular vinegar

Instructions

  1. Pickle your onions:
    In a jar, add your thinly sliced onions. Fill the jar with equal parts regular vinegar and apple cider vinegar. Let marinate for 2 days
  2. Cook ground pork:
    Add olive oil to a pan, keep heat on high and add ground pork. Cook until brown while adding Tex Mex seasoning periodically.
  3. Cook kale:
    In a separate pan, add olive oil and cook your damn kale.
  4. Serve:
    Finally, add your pork on to soft tacos, add your pickled onions and kale, and finally, top with feta cheese
